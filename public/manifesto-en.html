Science Legal Protocol

Manifesto on Scientific Externalities

AI industrializes research. Law must industrialize accountability.

We legalize not researchers' daily routines, but the rights, duties, risks, and correction pathways of scientific outputs once they affect society.

Ethics checklists alone cannot govern scaled externalities. We need auditable duties, clear liability boundaries, and executable remedies.

  1. Clear ownership and responsibility layers.
  2. Auditable evidence of due diligence.
  3. Actionable correction and remedy interfaces.
  4. Lower total compliance burden, not more bureaucracy.
  5. Portable records across funders, journals, and institutions.
