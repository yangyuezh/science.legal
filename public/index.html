What We Are

A legal infrastructure initiative for research outputs and AI-enabled science: clear ownership, responsibility boundaries, auditable due-diligence evidence, and correction/remedy interfaces.

What We Are Not

  • Not more approvals.
  • Not more forms.
  • Not more KPI pressure.

What We Deliver

  • Reusable compliance package across funders, journals, and institutions.
  • Versioned signatures and evidence anchors for accountability.
  • Appealable, correctable process after incidents.

Three Public Commitments

  1. Burden reduction: new requirements must reduce total workflow load.
  2. Portability: record once, reuse many times.
  3. Due process: accountability must be reviewable, correctable, and traceable.
